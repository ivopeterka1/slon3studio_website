Remote Mouse Privacy Policy

Remote Mouse does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app communicates only over your local network to connect your iPhone with your Mac companion application.

Data Used by the App

  • Local network connection information
  • Mouse and keyboard control commands sent between your devices

The App Does Not

  • Collect personal information
  • Track users
  • Use analytics services
  • Send data to external servers
  • Store user activity

All communication happens locally between your devices.
